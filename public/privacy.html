Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2024

Overview

PolaMoment is a camera application that creates vintage-style Polaroid photos on your iOS device. Your privacy is important to us.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Data Collection and Storage

What We Collect

  • Photos: All photos you capture are stored locally on your device only
  • Camera Access: Required to capture photos
  • Photo Library Access: Optional, only if you choose to save photos to your library

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information
  • We do NOT track your usage or analytics
  • We do NOT share data with third parties
  • We do NOT use cloud storage or servers
  • We do NOT require an account or login

Data Storage

All photos and app data remain exclusively on your device. The app functions entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

Permissions

Camera Permission

Required for the core functionality of capturing photos with vintage Polaroid-style effects.

Photo Library Permission

Optional permission to save your captured Polaroid-style photos to your device's photo library. You can use the app without granting this permission.

Children's Privacy

PolaMoment does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
